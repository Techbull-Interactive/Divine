About Us

The primary objectives of Divine Spectrum Sdn Bhd is to provide an extenuated and comprehensive range of Civil,
Mechanical & Electrical (Energy & Solar Power System) Construction, Telecommunication and Project Management capacities and expertise to specific projects as well as individual customers.

Our Services

MECHANICAL AND ELECTRICAL

Mechanical and Electrical (M&E) is another core business of Divine Spectrum in which we provide this service to Housing Development project, Commercial High-rise Building, Factory, Infrastructure, Airport, Medical Centre and Hotel.

RESOURCE & MANPOWER SOURCING

The company is structured to undertake full turnkey project, hence we have already trained technical engineers in 2G, 3G, Microwave, Optical Transmission and Wireless Broadband technology in different telecommunication vendors' equipment.

MANAGED & FIELD MAINTENANCE

Divine Spectrum has a proven track records and experiences to provide managed services and first level maintenance field support to cover all critical network elements including tower, cabin, power supply and etc. Figure 4 illustrate Divine Spectrum Managed Services Framework.

TELECOM EQUIPMENT

Provide major Telecommunication Vendors such as Ericsson, Nokia-Siemens, Alcatel-Lucent, NEC and Huawei in proving them the engineering services that related to their equipment such as base station and microwave with different technology GSM, 3G, PDH.

FULL TURNKEY DEPLOYMENT

Provide Full Turnkey Engineering services. Specifically, our solution encompasses network planning and design, site acquisition and permitting, site deployment, telecom implementation, network integration and operation and maintenance.
